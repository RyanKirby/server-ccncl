What to Expect

Who are we?

You can expect to meet ordinary people from different cultures and walks of life. There is no dress code, come as you would if you were visiting friends.

Kids

We offer secure gospel-centered programmes for children under the age of 13. Simply sign in your kids at the “check in” area and we will sort out the rest.

The Service

We generally start our time in worship to God. We love Jesus and love singing about and too him. We speak a lot about Jesus and we base what we say on Him.
