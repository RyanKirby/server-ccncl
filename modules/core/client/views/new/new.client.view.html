What to Expect

Who are we?

You can expect to meet ordinary people from different cultures and walks of life! There is no dress code, come as you would if you were visiting friends.

The Service

We usually start our time in musical worship to God, with a full band which leads us in contemporary worship songs. We then have someone teach us from the Bible to explain the relevance and beauty of the gospel message.

Kids

We offer secure gospel-centred programmes for children under the age of 13. Simply sign your kids in at the “Check In” area and we’ll sort out the rest!

Time & Place

Venue

We currently use the 1st floor of Bamburgh House on Market Street in the city centre of Newcastle Upon Tyne.
