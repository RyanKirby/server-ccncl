What is a GC?

Gospel communities are all about knowing Jesus and making Him known. They are communities of people that are centred on knowing, believing and living out the implication of the gospel together as a family in the day-to-day and the extraordinary.

They meet regularly in each others homes and we enjoy meals together, study the bible, disciple one another, look for opportunities to serve, bless and reach out to our broader neighbourhood with the gospel.
