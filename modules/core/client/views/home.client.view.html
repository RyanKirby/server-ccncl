Cornerstone

Cornerstone Church Newcastle is a gospel-centred, Spirit-led city church! We exist to know Jesus and make Jesus known in the city of Newcastle-upon-Tyne, as well as the surrounding areas in the North East of England. Whether you are young or old, student or professional, local or international we look forward to having you at our church this Sunday at 10:30am!
